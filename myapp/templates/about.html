About TravelMate

TravelMate is your trusted travel partner, connecting curious travelers with verified tour vendors across the country. Our goal is to make trip planning simple, secure, and enjoyable — from discovering dream destinations to booking with confidence.

We offer admin-approved tour packages, vendor-managed listings, and secure Razorpay payments to ensure that your travel experience starts off right.

Whether you're a traveler eager to explore, a vendor looking to share experiences, or an admin maintaining quality— TravelMate is here to guide your way.
